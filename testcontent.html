Institute for Foundational Learning, Inc.

Institute for Foundational Learning is a nonprofit private school school accredited by the Department of Education (DepEd) that provides quality education from preschool to high schoolfor children from the community and IFL children's home and godly character, producing men and women who will have transforming influence in their community, nation, and the world. Its mission is to provide quality education grounded in Christan faith, character, and values for the holistic development of learners who through their lives will impact society and culture. The essence of our mission is found in Proverbs 22:6, “Train up a child in the way he should go, and when he grows old he will not depart from it.”
